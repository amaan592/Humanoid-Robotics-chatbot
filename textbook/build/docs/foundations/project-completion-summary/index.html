Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook - Project Completion

Project Overview​

The Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics textbook project has been successfully completed, delivering a comprehensive graduate-level educational resource covering all specified requirements. The textbook encompasses 18 chapters across 6 core parts with integrated simulation-to-reality transfer concepts.

Completed Deliverables​

Textbook Structure​

  • 18 Core Chapters: Complete coverage from 01-18 across all parts
  • 6 Core Parts: Foundations, Sensors, ROS, Simulation, Locomotion, Embodiment
  • Supporting Materials: Standards, templates, validation frameworks
  • Technical Integration: Full integration of ROS 2, Gazebo, and NVIDIA Isaac

Chapter Distribution​

  • Part 1 - Foundations (01-03): Core Physical AI concepts and embodied intelligence
  • Part 2 - Sensors (04-06): Sensor systems, perception integration, and fusion
  • Part 3 - ROS (07-09): ROS 2 fundamentals, communication, and ecosystem
  • Part 4 - Simulation (10-12): Gazebo basics, URDF modeling, and digital twins
  • Part 5 - Locomotion (13-15): Locomotion systems, movement control, and kinematics
  • Part 6 - Embodiment (16-18): Autonomous control, interaction, and integration

Technical Stack Integration​

  • ROS 2: Full integration across all relevant chapters
  • Gazebo Simulation: Comprehensive coverage with physics accuracy
  • NVIDIA Isaac: Perception and AI integration throughout
  • Simulation-to-Reality Transfer: Integrated in all relevant chapters
